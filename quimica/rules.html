Rules & Regulations



General Rules

  • Only participants with valid identity card are eligible to participate.
  • Any electronic instruments or use of mobile phones are prohibited.
  • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges and organizers shall be final and abiding.

Chem Shoot

  • The teams need to prepare a layout and detailed idea regarding the chem shoot.
  • The details must be submitted beforehand to the organizers before 10 days of the event.
  • Links related to aqua rocket are available at the website and they can refer it for assistance
  • Launchpads will be prepared for the participants only after submission of their detailed idea.
  • Any one member of the team need to describe the parts, construction, uses and need to answer the queries if asked by the judge.
  • Then from the launchpads the rockets need to be launched.
  • The team whose rocket will cover the maximum distance will be declared as winner.
