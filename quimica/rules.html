Rules & Regulations



General Rules

  • Only participants with valid identity card are eligible to participate.
  • Any electronic instruments or use of mobile phones are prohibited.
  • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges and organizers shall be final and abiding.

Chem Shoot

  • The teams need to prepare a layout and detailed idea regarding the chem shoot.
  • The details must be submitted beforehand to the organizers before 10 days of the event.
  • Links related to aqua rocket are available at the website and they can refer it for assistance
  • Launchpads will be prepared for the participants only after submission of their detailed idea.
  • Any one member of the team need to describe the parts, construction, uses and need to answer the queries if asked by the judge.
  • Then from the launchpads the rockets need to be launched.
  • The team whose rocket will cover the maximum distance will be declared as winner.

CASCON

  • The teams will be provided with 2 daily life issues related to chemical engineering field.
  • The issues will be given to the teams before 15 days.
  • The teams need to take a detailed study over these issues.
  • The teams need to select any one of the issues.
  • They need to prepare a detailed presentation over the selected issues.
  • The members of the team need to present themselves well by firstly introducing the selected issue, way of finding solutions and solving the solutions.
  • They need to suggest a most effective, economic and easily accessible idea related to it.
  • Any type of sources can be utilized.
  • The sources utilized in the idea must be designated in the power point.

CHEMCRUISE

  • The teams have to build a car upto their own specifications
  • They need to build a car of uniform cross section with better economic aspects
  • They need to prepare a fuel which is non pollutant and eco-friendly.
  • The car needs to cover a certain distance with a safe speed.
  • The teams need to provide a detailed idea before performing their car’s performance.
  • The winner will be declared completely based on the utility of the car.
  • The participant must show their ideas beforehand.
  • Prizes: - 1st, 2nd and 3rd rank holding teams will be awarded with cash prizes and certificates.

CHEM ADS

  • Only participants with valid identity card are eligible to participate.
  • The size of the poster must be 23’X33’(A1) or more than that.
  • We suggest that in your abstract you use headings as ‘introduction’ ,’methods’ ,’results’ and ‘conclusions’ to identify your poster layout.
  • The decision of the judges and organizers shall be final and abiding.
  • Also include an acknowledgement section, containing details of anyone/any source who helped you conduct this project.
  • Word limits for submitting the abstract is 120-150, which must be submitted to the organiser before 5 days of the event, well mentioning the name and institute of the participant.
  • No group participation will be entertained in this event.
  • Prizes: - winner will be awarded with the cash prizes with certificate and runners up will be awarded with certificate.

Quizzically

Win it through pen (Round 1):

Show your calibre with pen. Make an unbreakable combination of pen and your brain to win the round and to step ahead.
  • Rules and Regulations:
  • 30 questions will be displayed on the screen.
  • Total marks of the round 1 is 50.
  • There will be 3 levels of questions.
    • Level 1: - consists of 16 questions each carrying 1 mark.
    • Level 2: - consists of 12 questions each carrying 2 marks.
    • Level 3: - consists of 2 questions each carrying 5 marks.
  • Time limit for
    • Level 1: - 2 mins
    • Level 2: - 5 mins
    • Level 3: - 8 mins
  • Teams will be provided with a pen and paper.
  • Questions will be repeated for verification of answers.
  • Questions of level 1 and 2 will be MCQs based and of level 3 will be plot based.
  • Only 8 teams will be qualified for the next round.
  • Rapido fire will be organized for the tiebreaker.

Groupie (Round 2):

Think before making team. A combination of 2 can make you through to finals or if played individually face the consequence. Team effort is the must.
  • Rules and Regulations:
  • There will be 2 sessions of group discussions.
  • Each session of GD will consist of 8 students (1 member from each team).
  • The winner of GD will be based on combined marks of both members of team.
  • Only 3 teams will qualify for the final round.

Pass to win cash (Round 3):

After passing through tough roads time is for your team to make it worth by converting your calibre into real money.
  • Rules and Regulations:
  • Each member of the team will face the interview.
  • Individual marks will be summed up to give us the winner.
  • The winner of GD will be based on combined marks of both members of team.
  • Questions will be based on general and basic chemical engineering topics.

Note: - There will be 3 prizes. Winner of round 2 will definitely be the prize holders. But if the team doesn’t appear the round 3 then that team will be debarred of getting prize.
