ABOUT US

QUIMICA the grandiose od chemical fiestas, is a three day techno-cultural extravaganza,being successfully organized for last three years by the department od chemical engineering IGIT, Sarang. This fest benefits the budding chemical engineers from all over the nation as it not only highlights their hidden talents but also provides a grand platform to showcase it to the world. From the grand success we got from Quimica 4.0, we are now going to present you it's fifth edition with gaiety and fervour. There was a huge participation of students from various premier technical and cultural as well as fun events.

Apart from these, the two social nights were icing on the cake. Last time we achieved all that by finding a team who always had each other's back. So, we take this opportunity to thank every single team member who imbibed the spirit of Quimica and made it a grand success.
