Rules & Regulations



CIC - CAC - COE

About:-
Got a good observations skill, give it a go. Its principle based on the classic tic tac toe but with a little twist. Solving the chemical reaction is what you got to do. Keep your opponent in check before they take any step.

  • Rules and Regulations:
  • A tic tac toe set will be provided to the players.
  • In a match only 2 players will play against each other.
  • The players will be provided with blank dice boxes.
  • They need to write the desired chemical elements and compounds on the dices.
  • The players need to arrange the complete reactions including the reactants and products.
  • The player who completes the whole reaction at first will be declared as the winner of that match.
  • The whole event will be knockout based.

CHEMCHARADES

About:-
It is for the students who loves to act. It is their thing to boast about. And playing with gestures and hand movement is what all you need to do. But with a little touch of chemical accent. So, do not mismatch your movement.

  • Rules and Regulations:
  • There will be a team of 4 members.
  • Each team will be provided with 2 questions.
  • For each match, there will be 2 teams facing each other.
  • Each team will give a question to one of the members of the other team.
  • That member has to solve that question and enact it to its own team to get the answer.
  • The whole activity needs to be completed in 5 mins.
  • All the teams have to pass by through the knockout stages to be the winner.

BE WEIRD, EXIHIBIT!

About:-
Are you Photogenic? Then, this event is perfect for you. All you need to do is click as much as weirdest pictures, which will be put into exhibition show. And the winner will be decided based on their photography skills. Also, those pictures will be showcased into the department. So, it is a big deal to click a weirder than others.

SENSE - BOWL - A - THON

About:-
Six sense, it is easy to make sense. It will test your ability to sense. All your sensory organs will be put under the pressure of respective their performance. Students will be blind folded and guess the chemical. Based on their right guess they will have to throw the darts onto the accurate positions. So, keep your head straight, for your throw to be clickbait.

  • Rules and Regulations:
  • There will be a team of 3 members each.
  • There will be a dish plate containing any one of the chemicals.
  • One of the team members will have to smell the item and need to answer it to the organizer and to the teammate.
  • The 2nd member has to speak about the chemical compounds correctly.
  • Then, the 3rd member will have to hit the dart of that particular chemicals to the spin wheel containing the names of the chemicals.
  • The team which clears all the rounds will be declared as winner.
  • The participant must show their ideas beforehand.

BUNGING BALLOONS

About:-
As the name suggests, balloons are fun to play with. Bunging balloons will entertain the students. Students will be divided into teams. And the winner will be decided based on the highest numbers of balloons they will collect from one end to the other. Give it a try, because you are never too old to play.

  • Rules and Regulations:
  • A team of 2 members can participate.
  • There will be a box full of balloons.
  • There will be another empty box and a seating chair.
  • The 1st member has to pick up the balloon from the box and carry it to the empty box with hands tied.
  • The participant is not allowed to use its legs.
  • The balloon should not touch the ground and burst too.
  • The balloons collected in the empty box will be collected by 2nd member through hands and need to burst it with the help of its bum only.
  • The team needs to complete the whole activity in 3 mins only.
  • The team with maximum number of burst balloons will be declared as winner.
